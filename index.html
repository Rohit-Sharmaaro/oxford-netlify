Top Oxford Attractions to Visit on a Sightseeing Tour by Coach

Oxford is home to a treasure trove of historical sites and landmarks, many of which are best explored via coach. Here are the must-see attractions you can visit on your tour:

The University of Oxford is one of the world’s oldest and most prestigious institutions, and its colleges are architectural masterpieces. Some of the must-visit colleges include Christ Church, Magdalen College, and Balliol College. These are all easily accessible by coach, so you can spend less time traveling and more time exploring.

Don’t miss out on Oxford’s cultural offerings, including the Oxford University Museum of Natural History and the Botanic Garden. These spots offer a unique glimpse into Oxford’s scientific and natural heritage, and they’re perfect for a leisurely stroll during your sightseeing tour.
